About The Orange Alliance

The Orange Alliance is a site to centeralize FIRST Tech Challenge Scores so that all Teams may get access to data.


The Orange Alliance was inspired by The Blue Alliance, but we are not affiliated.

Meet The Core Devs of The Orange Alliance

Alexander Fera Project Manager

Alex is the Orange Alliance Project Manager, Lead FIRST Technical Advisor for FIRST in Michigan for both FIRST Robotics Competiton and FIRST Tech Challenge. On the TOA app Alex concentrates on Database Architecture, Data storage and Data import. He is also one of the Developers for FTC's Official Scoring System. Alex is the Owner of his own IT Consulting Firm, Fera Group, LLC, based in Northern Michigan and is 10 year volunteer with FIRST.

Kyle Flynn Developer in Chief

Kyle Flynn is the lead developer for The Orange Alliance. He develops and maintains the core functionality of The Orange Alliance as well as the public API. Spending 4 years as a student on an FRC team, Kyle is now a 2nd year FIRST in Michigan volunteer as both a FIRST Technical Advisor for FTC, and a FIRST Technical Advisor Assistant/Control System Advisor for FRC. Kyle is also studying Computer Science and Business Administration at Grand Valley State University.
